Hi! This Web page belongs to the person pictured on the right. I'm a freelance writer, editor, copywriter, and translator of fiction, poetry, and short stories, based in Princeton, NJ, and Brooklyn, NY.

As a translator and writer, I draw on psychoanalysis and the prophetic/mystic traditions (Jewish/Black/Islamic) in an attempt to find a diagnostic/remedy for our contemporary situation and a (personal / collective) path to action.
