Biography

I have a BA degree from Yale University. After graduating from Yale in 2020, I taught at the English Department of the Chinese University of Hong Kong as a Visiting Tutor. From 2022 till 2024, I was an associate at the D. E. Shaw group, a global investment and technology development firm, where I worked as a ghostwriter, copyeditor, and fact-checker.

Writing

I translate literary, psychoanalytic, philosophical, and religious texts to and from Spanish, English, French, and Catalan. Please refer to my translations page for information about my works in progress.
